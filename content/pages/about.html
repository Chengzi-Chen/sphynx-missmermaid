Selective. Ethical. Artistic.

Miss Mermaid Sphynx was born backstage at fashion week, where our founder noticed models and creators magnetised to the regal confidence of a visiting Sphynx companion. Today we honour that spark with a boutique cattery that treats every kitten like a living sculpture destined for a discerning home.

Selective Pairings

We orchestrate no more than four litters annually, blending CFA Grand Champions with emerging prodigies who demonstrate affectionate eye contact, responsive vocalisations, and relaxed handling. Pedigree data is traced for ten generations, and our mentors in Our Cats annotate every nuance, from paw temperature to cuddle stamina.

Ethical Foundations

Transparency anchors our guardianship pledge. Prospective families tour our climate-zoned nursery virtually or in person, meet our veterinary technician, and receive detailed wellness metrics. We extend lifetime conversations, linking you with the Care Guide practice and our dedicated concierge desk on the Contact page.

Artistic Stewardship

Each environment is curated with tactile textiles, tonal palettes, and scent therapy that complements their hairless physique. Our collaborative artists capture their elegance for the Gallery, while bespoke knitwear designers keep bodies warm between spotlight moments.

Origin Story

Founder Mireya "Miss Mermaid" Solis apprenticed with European catteries before establishing our house in the Pacific Northwest. Inspired by sculptural water choreography, she designed habitats with floating platforms, warm airflow, and soothing playlists. These elements remain central to Wanda, Yoda, Dada, and Gogoda’s daily routines.

Our guardianship council includes a cardiologist monitoring hypertrophic cardiomyopathy markers, a feline nutritionist, and a behaviourist specialising in confidence training. Every specialist contributes to our adoption dossiers so families are never left guessing.

Differentiators

  • Immersive Orientation: Families receive a 90-minute onboarding covering bathing choreography, temperature staging, and travel preparation with direct links to the FAQ suite.
  • Concierge Wellness: Quarterly alumni salons feature case studies from the Testimonials gallery highlighting thriving matches.
  • Art Partnerships: Each kitten leaves with a miniature portrait and access to our digital styling library, curated alongside the Gallery team.
  • Holistic Nutrition: Our recipes blend raw and gently cooked menus tailored to each guardian’s lifestyle, supported by the nutrition strategies outlined in the Care Guide.
