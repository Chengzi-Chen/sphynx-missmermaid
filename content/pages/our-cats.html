House Rituals & Guardian Pathways

Our ensemble thrives on predictability paired with creative enrichment. Guardians receive weekly rehearsal notes covering hydration, climate adjustments, and socialisation prompts. Use these alongside the Care Guide to create a nurturing environment that mirrors our studio.

  • Monday: Aroma therapy check-in followed by tactile play led by Wanda.
  • Wednesday: Interactive puzzle circuits inspired by Dada’s favourite obstacle course.
  • Friday: Velvet lounge storytelling with Gogoda, paired with mindfulness stretches from Yoda.

Families considering adoption can observe these rituals during virtual tours and replicate the cadence at home prior to kitten arrival. The deeper your alignment with our rhythm, the smoother your transition into lifelong guardianship.
