How to Experience the Collections

  • Desktop: Use full-screen mode for detailed texture studies and compare colour grading between show lighting and studio ambiance.
  • Mobile: Enable portrait orientation for everyday vignettes and landscape for show captures to enjoy proportional cropping.
  • Chromecast & AirPlay: Stream the slideshow to curate ambience during gatherings or pre-adoption consultations.

Each gallery set is optimised for fast loading via Imagify compression while preserving tonal depth suited to our black-and-gold aesthetic. Should you need high-resolution files for press or large-format printing, note the image ID and request it via the Contact concierge.

We refresh the collections every quarter and archive seasonal favourites within alumni newsletters. Share your home vignettes through the Testimonials form for a chance to be featured in Everyday Joy.

Curation Notes & Commission Process

Every collection is storyboarded by our creative director in partnership with alumni families. We chart lighting, texture, and mood before the shoot, ensuring each image conveys the warmth central to Miss Mermaid. Guardians receive mood boards ahead of time so wardrobes and props complement the black-and-gold aesthetic.

Collectors or brands seeking bespoke visuals can commission limited-series sessions. Share your concept, intended medium, and timeline via the Contact concierge. We pair you with our art team, secure studio time, and craft a usage plan covering gallery rights, social media, and print campaigns.

  • Portfolio Review: Receive sample galleries categorized by lighting style and subject focus.
  • Pre-Production Call: Align on story arc, wardrobe palette, and NextGEN presentation format.
  • Delivery: Access high-resolution files, optimized web sets, and alt text suggestions to support accessibility.

We refresh NextGEN galleries quarterly, archiving previous seasons in Rank Math-controlled sitemap segments for SEO continuity. Subscribe to the newsletter on the Home page to catch behind-the-scenes releases before public launch.
