Our Five-Step Adoption Journey

Every placement is a curated partnership. We pace the journey with care checkpoints, transparent communication, and personalised coaching.

  • 01 · Introduce — Apply with lifestyle details and habitat snapshots.
  • 02 · Align — Virtual nursery tour and guardianship interview.
  • 03 · Reserve — Confirm match with contract and wellness blueprint.
  • 04 · Prepare — Tailored supply kit, nutrition planning, and vet introductions.
  • 05 · Welcome — Homecoming ceremony, post-arrival check-ins, and alumni access.

What to expect next

Your coordinator delivers weekly updates, ensures wellness milestones are met, and prepares your kitten for a stress-free transition.
