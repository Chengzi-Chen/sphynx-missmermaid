Adoption with Heart

Our guardianship journey is paced so your household feels ready, resourced, and supported. We remain beside you from initial inquiry through lifelong alumni salons.

1. Curated Application

Share your home rhythm, travel cadence, and existing companions. We review your narrative alongside resources in the Care Guide to tailor recommendations.

2. Alignment Conversation

Meet our guardianship concierge via video tour. We showcase nursery zones, introduce Wanda or Yoda, and align expectations on grooming, nutrition, and budget.

3. Reservation Harmony

Once aligned, reserve your kitten with a signed guardianship charter. You receive weekly updates, scent swatches, and enrichment assignments.

4. Environment Rehearsal

We coach you on temperature zoning, skincare rituals, and emergency prep using templates from the FAQ archive and Care Guide.

5. Homecoming & Alumni Care

Your kitten travels with a couture wardrobe, nutrition plan, and veterinary dossier. Post-arrival, we schedule check-ins at one week, one month, and three months, plus invite you to share updates on the Testimonials lounge.

Support Timeline & Guardian Touchpoints

From the moment your inquiry lands, our concierge documents every preference—ambient music, wardrobe styles, travel plans—and folds them into a personalised guardianship roadmap. Use this overview alongside the Care Guide to pace your household preparations.

  • Week 0: Application review, lifestyle alignment call, and curated reading list.
  • Week 2: Nursery livestream, temperature audit checklist, and grooming kit recommendations.
  • Week 4: Reservation confirmation, scent exchange, and virtual rehearsal featuring Wanda or Yoda.
  • Week 6: Homecoming logistics, travel carriers, and emergency contact protocols.
  • Post-arrival: Scheduled check-ins at days 7, 30, and 90 plus alumni salon invitation.

These milestones stay flexible; families can request additional sessions focused on nutrition, multi-pet introductions, or international travel. We map each appointment using Rank Math notes so nothing is missed.
