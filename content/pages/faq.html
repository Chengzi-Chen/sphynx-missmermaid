Frequently Asked Questions

Curated answers gathered from guardian conversations. Continue exploring the adoption story on the Adoption page and study deeper care rituals via the Care Guide.

How often should I bathe my Sphynx?

Plan weekly spa rituals using lukewarm water and fragrance-free cleansers. Supplement with midweek wipe-downs. Refer to the Skin & Bathing section in our Care Guide for product recommendations and step-by-step choreography.

Do Sphynx cats get cold easily?

They enjoy warmth between 72°F and 80°F. Layer knitwear, provide thermal mats, and schedule sun time responsibly. The Temperature chapter outlines zoning tips.

What diet do you recommend?

Four to five micro-meals blending raw and gently cooked recipes with hydration support. Explore ratios and supplements in the Nutrition Architecture section.

How do you screen for HCM?

We partner with board-certified cardiologists for annual echocardiograms. Adoptive families receive scan records and reminders in our newsletter. See the Health & HCM Awareness notes.

Can I meet the kittens before adoption?

Yes, via virtual studio tours or curated in-person visits. Schedule through the Contact page after completing the Adoption Inquiry.

What comes in the adoption kit?

Wardrobe pieces, nutrition plan, vet dossier, scent blanket, and access to alumni salons. Preview the full list on the Adoption page.

Are Sphynx cats hypoallergenic?

No cat is fully hypoallergenic, but our grooming rituals and nutrition reduce dander. We coach you on environmental prep in the Care Guide.

How do you socialise kittens?

Daily cuddle rehearsals, sound desensitisation, and structured play led by Wanda and Yoda. Watch snippets in the Gallery.

Do you offer post-adoption support?

Absolutely. We host check-ins at one week, one month, and quarterly thereafter. Alumni receive invite-only tutorials and can share stories within the Testimonials lounge.

How do I reserve a kitten?

Start with the Adoption Inquiry form, review the guardianship charter, and place your reservation. The Adoption page walks through each step.

What if I need travel guidance?

Our concierge provides bespoke travel kits, cabin acclimation routines, and vet paperwork. Reach out via the Contact portal for personalised support.

What kind of support do you offer international adopters?

We provide bilingual liaisons, time zone friendly check-ins, and custom travel dossiers. Our concierge partners with your veterinarian to manage import requirements and acclimation plans. Use the Contact portal to request a tailored briefing.

How do you prepare kittens for children or other pets?

Kittens practice gentle handling with supervised play and confidence-building exercises guided by our behaviourist. Families receive integration scripts sourced from the Care Guide so introductions feel calm and celebratory.

Do you offer boarding or retreat services?

Yes. Alumni can reserve spots at our wellness retreat featuring climate-controlled suites, daily enrichment classes, and live video updates. Contact us via the Contact page to check availability.
