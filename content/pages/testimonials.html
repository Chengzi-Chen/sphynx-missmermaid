Stories from Our Velvet Alumni

Every tale reflects the adoption choreography described on the Adoption page and the daily rituals mastered through our Care Guide.

The Atelier Loft · Wanda x Elise

Elise, a textile artist, says Wanda’s presence transformed her studio: “She perches on the loom bench, eyes half-closed, listening to jazz as if critiquing every warp and woof. The Miss Mermaid team coached me on humidifying the space without damaging fabric, and Wanda rewards us with twilight cuddle critiques.”

Elise adds that the concierge follow-ups helped her integrate seasonal nutrition tweaks and align Wanda’s skincare schedule with photoshoot demands. “Their notes bridged the gap between artistic intuition and clinical expertise,” she explains.

The Skyline Home · Yoda x Amir & Devon

Amir and Devon live in a high-rise overlooking the bay. “Yoda wakes us for sunrise yoga, stretching with perfect form. The concierge team’s travel kit made cabin flights effortless. We now mentor new guardians by sharing our routines in the alumni salon.”

They host quarterly virtual gatherings where alumni exchange playlists, thermal layering tips, and enrichment ideas inspired by Yoda’s mindfulness sessions. “The community feels like a design collective dedicated to feline wellbeing,” Amir notes.

The Garden Estate · Dada & Gogoda x Tran Family

The Tran family adopted bonded duo Dada and Gogoda. “They sprint through our botanical greenhouse and collapse beneath the radiant floor heating. Miss Mermaid’s nutrition counsel keeps their energy balanced, and the gallery portrait session captured their sibling choreography beautifully.”

Their children helped craft enrichment mazes modelled after the Our Cats profiles. “Miss Mermaid encouraged us to document successes and speed bumps so future families can iterate faster,” the family shares.
