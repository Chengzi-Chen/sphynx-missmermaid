Concierge Highlights for First-Time Visitors

Begin with a private discovery call where we review lifestyle rhythms and align expectations. You will receive a personalised dossier summarising favourite enrichment, room temperature guidance, and links to the Care Guide so you can prepare tactile comforts before your kitten arrives.

Our welcome sequence also includes a digital mood board featuring knitwear palettes, scent therapy cues, and playlists that follow the cadence of our nursery. Families often print these visuals to create inspiration corners at home. Once you confirm interest, we schedule a virtual walk-through that introduces each resident and explains how we storyboard daily enrichment. The follow-up email contains direct access to the FAQ suite and alumni testimonials so you can review real-world experiences while planning your own.

For returning guardians, our concierge offers seasonal refreshers highlighting new nutrition discoveries, recommended travel accessories, and early notice of upcoming litters. These updates ensure your home evolves alongside your Sphynx companion’s needs, keeping artistry and wellbeing in harmony.
